of the approved long-lived design baseline.

Place material here when:

- a design direction is being explored
- terminology or boundaries are still unstable
- a future phase needs shaping before promotion into `docs/design`

Move a document out of this directory when:

- the design is approved as a stable baseline and belongs in `docs/design`
- the design remains accepted but deferred and is better represented by a
  roadmap placeholder in `docs/future-plans`
